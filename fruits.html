About Us

Nestled in the heart of Little Tokyo in Los Angeles, CA, Little Tokyo Florist is an AAPI Japanese American-owned floral business. Our passion lies in creating exquisite and authentic Japanese Ikebana designs, showcasing the time-honored art form with a modern twist. Whether you're seeking a vibrant bouquet for a special occasion or a personalized arrangement to brighten someone's day, we are dedicated to providing you with the finest blooms and exceptional service.


The owner, Yuka Mizusawa, is a vibrant florist with a keen eye and unique designs. With a passion for blooms, she infuses every arrangement with lively colors and contagious enthusiasm, turning any visit into a delightful floral experience.
